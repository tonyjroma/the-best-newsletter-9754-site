Six ways to bend a room

Alternating warm woods and playful retro color hits—each piece transforms when you need it to.

Wall‑mounted folding desk

Folds flat when you’re done, reveals a built‑in monitor when you’re on. Walnut, brass, and a soft cream work surface.

Coffee → dining table

Low and quiet until dinner. Lifts to full dining height, with hidden drawers stocked with lobster bibs.

Storage bench → hat rack

Seat by day, entryway hero by night. The top lifts and locks into a vertical hat rack with hooks and a shelf.

Chair → step stool

A clean side chair that flips into a sturdy step stool when you need to reach the top shelf.

Nesting tables with speakers

Three nesting side tables with speakers built into their sides—separate them for surround sound, stack them when you’re done.

Expandable console table

Lives as a slim hallway console, then pulls and folds out into a full dining or work surface.
